## 2. The Three Core Content Types

### Content Type 1: House Listing Tour Video
**Purpose:** Create cinematic, drone-style property tour videos from still photographs.

**Key Features:**
- Vertical 9:16 format optimized for Reels/TikTok
- Ken Burns effect with smooth pans, zooms, and parallax motion
- AI-generated narration script describing property highlights
- Text-to-speech voiceover with multiple voice options
- Synchronized on-screen text overlays (price, features, address)
- Background music matching the property vibe (luxury, cozy, modern)
- Professional transitions between scenes (cross-dissolve, whip pan, slide)

**Output:** 15-60 second MP4 video with optional SRT/VTT subtitles

### Content Type 2: Listing Promotion / Event Video (Realtor On-Camera)
**Purpose:** Create personalized promo videos featuring the agent.

**Two Modes:**
1. **AI Avatar Mode:** Generate a realistic talking-head video from a single headshot photo
2. **Selfie Enhancement Mode:** Transform raw selfie footage into polished content with B-roll, captions, and graphics

**Use Cases:**
- Open house announcements
- "Just Listed" / "Just Sold" celebrations
- Neighborhood spotlights
- Market updates
- Personal brand building

**Key Features:**
- AI-scripted hooks and talking points
- Automatic B-roll insertion (property photos play while agent talks)
- Dynamic captions and text overlays
- Logo watermarks and branded lower-thirds
- Audio cleanup (denoise, normalize, remove dead air)

**Output:** 15-60 second MP4 with agent appearing on-screen

### Content Type 3: Infographic Post (Static or Animated)
**Purpose:** Create branded graphics summarizing property details.

**Formats:**
- Single-card image
- 3-card carousel
- 5-card carousel
- Animated story (light motion graphics)

**Content Elements:**
- Property hero image
- Price, beds, baths, sq ft
- Key features (pool, renovated kitchen, etc.)
- Open house dates/times
- Agent photo, name, contact info
- Brokerage logo and branding

**Output:** PNG images or short looping MP4 with subtle animations

## 3. Key Value Propositions

### For Individual Agents:
1. **Time Savings:** Create professional content in 5 minutes vs. 5 hours of manual editing
2. **Consistency:** Maintain a polished, on-brand social presence without design skills
3. **Cost Reduction:** Eliminate need for videographers, editors, and graphic designers
4. **Speed to Market:** List a property and have social content ready the same day
5. **Professional Quality:** AI-optimized compositions that look agency-produced

### For Teams & Brokerages:

2. **Template Libraries:** Share winning templates across the organization

### Competitive Advantages:
1. **Real Estate Specific:** Purpose-built for property marketing (not generic video tools)
2. **End-to-End Solution:** From photos to published post in one platform
3. **AI-First Approach:** Every step enhanced by AI (scripts, compositions, layouts)
4. **Template Intelligence:** Templates that adapt to different property types
5. **Multi-Platform Optimization:** Auto-adjust for each social platform's requirements

## 7. Compliance & Safety Requirements

### Content Safety:
- No generation of discriminatory language (Fair Housing Act compliance)
- No misleading claims about properties
- No invented property details (only use provided information)
- Professional, neutral real estate language

### Data Privacy:
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
- User data isolated by tenant
- Secure media storage with encryption
- Right to deletion for all user data

### Platform Compliance:
- Content meets Instagram/Facebook community guidelines
- Music library with proper licensing
- No copyright infringement in templates
